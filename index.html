CAERUS IS THE PERSONIFICATION OF OPPORTUNITY

In Greek mythology, Caerus (Kairos in Greek) was the youngest child of Zeus and the personification of opportunity and favorable moments. Nonetheless, Caerus is one of the four Ancient Greek rhetoric terms: logos, ethos, pathos and kairos, the timing element. Likewise, it is these values of opportunity and timing that represent the spirit of our fund.

Caerus Capital is one of the leading student investment groups in the B&R Beurs Community, based in Rotterdam as a part of the Erasmus University. The fund was established in 2016 by seventeen eager MSc Finance & Investment students, who shared a mutual interest in portfolio management and financial markets in general. Since the start the fund has consistently been a top performer in the Flowtraders Investment Competition, ultimately finishing second in the 2016-2017 edition with a return of 9.43% and a risk-adjusted return of 11.30%.

This year, we aim to top the ranking by adopting an increasingly sophisticated, professional approach. This year the fund will be led by a more diverse group of high achieving students, some of which are business oriented and others with quantitative backgrounds. Additionally, we will be partnering up with some professionals from the hedge fund industry, giving us another competitive edge.
